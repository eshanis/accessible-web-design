Accessibility Statement for FitnessEdventure

Fitness Edventure is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Measures to support accessibility

Fitness Edventure takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of FitnessEd:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement.
  • Include accessibility throughout our internal policies.
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff.
  • Assign clear accessibility targets and responsibilities.
  • Include people with disabilities in our design personas.
  • We will be evaluating our site on a regular basis and will continue to evolve and improve over time.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. FitnessEd is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Compatibility with browsers and assistive technology

FitnessEd is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Recent versions of popular screen readers
  • Operating system screen magnifiers
  • Speech recognition softeware

FitnessEd is not compatible with:

  • Archived documents: might not work with current assistive technologies because they use outdated technologies that do not support accessibility. We convert documents to new formats upon request within 5 business days. Please contact documents@fitnessEd.ml for support.
  • Browsers older than 3 major versions" or "Mobile operating systems older than 5 year

Technical specifications

Accessibility of FitnessEd relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.
